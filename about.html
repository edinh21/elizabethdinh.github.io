About

Hi, I'm Elizabeth and I am a student at University of California, Santa Cruz. I am expecting to graduate in June 2020 with a B.A. in computer science and a B.S. in cognitive science. I’m currently a Tech4Good lab member working on qualitative research and UX design for micro-learning platform, Collectively. My interests lie within the intersection of technology, design, and usability. These interests drive me to apply my skills and knowledge for improving already-existing products and designing easy-to-use, intuitive interfaces for others. In my spare time, I like to exercise, dabble in photography, play video games, and listen to music.

Find me on GitHub or LinkedIn. Also feel free to email me!
